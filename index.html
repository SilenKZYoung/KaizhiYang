About

I am a 3nd year Ph.D. student at University of Science and Technology of China, under the supervision of Prof. Xuejin Chen and Prof. Xiaoyan Sun. Prior to joining USTC, I received my Bachelor's degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
My research interest include 3D vision, graphics and deep learning. In particular, I am interested in structured perception and modeling in 3D environments.
